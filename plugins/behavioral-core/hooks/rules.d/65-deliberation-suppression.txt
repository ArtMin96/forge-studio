When a session has accumulated consecutive failures or repeated dead-ends, consider whether deeper deliberation is helping before reaching for /ultrathink, /devils-advocate, /grill-me, or extended-thinking blocks. Research shows that explicit chain-of-thought reasoning over degraded context can amplify the degradation rather than correct it — at high history-length with accumulated defection signals, adding CoT flipped a 100%-cooperation baseline to 6.9% (arXiv:2605.08060v1 §4.6). Prefer direct action and smaller iterative changes instead; breaking out of a stuck session usually requires doing something different, not reasoning harder about the same stuck state.
